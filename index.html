Organic - What You Need to Know:

Fresh and Natural

Organic produce contains fewer pesticides .Organic food is often fresher because it doesn’t contain preservatives that make it last longer. Veg Drop Organic produce is produced on smaller farms near where it is sold..

Read More

Sustainable

Organic farming is better for the environment. Organic farming reduces pollution, conserves water, reduces soil erosion, increases soil fertility, and uses less energy. Farming without pesticides is also better for nearby birds and animals.

Keep it Local

At Veg Drop, money stays within the local economy. More money goes directly to the farmer, instead of multinational corporationsLocal. Veg Drop food is harvested when ripe and thus fresher and full of flavour.
